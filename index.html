About Us

EduFourU is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young minds with the skills they need to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.

We specialize in teaching coding, mathematics, and journalism to children, providing them with the tools to think critically, solve problems, and express themselves creatively.

Our mission is to make high-quality education accessible to all, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to explore their potential and build a brighter future.

We foster learning through engaging, interactive, and accessible online workshops designed to inspire and empower young minds.
